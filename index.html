Steve Jobs

Co-founder of Apple • Visionary • Innovator

About Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur, inventor, and business leader. He was the co-founder of Apple Inc. and played a key role in revolutionizing the technology industry with innovative products like the iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

Life Details

  • Born: February 24, 1955
  • Birthplace: San Francisco, California, USA
  • Died: October 5, 2011
  • Education: Reed College (Dropped out)
  • Company: Apple Inc., Pixar, NeXT

Major Achievements

Co-founded Apple Inc. (1976)
Created the Macintosh Computer
Introduced iPhone (2007)
Revolutionized animation with Pixar
Changed the smartphone industry

Famous Quote

“Stay hungry, stay foolish.”
